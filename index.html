Founded 2024 · Ongoing

Bridging food waste and malnutrition — from a classroom to the UN.

Annadatri is a student-led initiative I founded to connect surplus food with NGOs in real-time. The technology platform, Ahaavritti, is built with React and Firebase, and became the subject of a filed patent and two Scopus-indexed papers.

Recognised in the Top 40 of Samsung Solve for Tomorrow at IIT Delhi and presented at the United Nations in Geneva, where I was named a Top 50 Global Changemaker.

About Me

The chapters of my story

Chapter 1: My Story

I am Anusha — a poet, researcher, and front-end developer. I work at the intersection of narrative and interface, combining creative writing with human-centered design to build meaningful digital experiences and community-focused projects.

My practice blends qualitative research, iterative prototyping, and thoughtful visual design. I founded Annadatri, a student-led initiative addressing food waste and malnutrition, and I contribute to research and community programs that emphasize measurable social impact.

I approach projects with curiosity, empathy, and a focus on accessibility and scale — balancing poetic expression with practical engineering.

Chapter 2: My Strengths

  • Creative Writing & Poetry
  • Front-End Development (HTML / CSS / JavaScript)
  • User Interface & Visual Design
  • Research & Community Organizing
  • Leadership & Collaboration

Chapter 3: Hobbies

Outside projects, I pursue creative and intellectual hobbies that feed both imagination and craft. Below are the practices I return to again and again.

  • Cooking: I experiment with seasonal and regional dishes — cooking is a creative lab where technique, patience, and curiosity meet.
  • Researching: I enjoy evidence-driven inquiry, literature reviews, and applied research for social impact projects.
  • Reading (Classics): Classics are my favorite — I read widely across modern and classic literature to deepen storytelling, vocabulary, and perspective.
  • Writing Poetry: I write regularly, workshop with peers, and explore forms that connect personal reflection to broader social themes.
  • Learning Coding Lessons (Professional): I pursue structured, professional learning in front-end development — accessibility, testing, modern frameworks, and practical projects for resumes and open-source contributions.

The Changemaker Journey

From a local initiative to the United Nations

1

2024: Founding Annadatri

Launched Annadatri, a student-led initiative focused on bridging the gap between food waste and malnutrition. This was the foundation of my mission to create a zero-hunger community.

2

The Evolution: Ahaarvritti Web App

To scale the impact, I developed Ahaarvritti—a dedicated web application designed to connect donors with excess food directly to NGOs in real-time. This technical solution was recognized in the Top 40 of Samsung Solve for Tomorrow (SFT) at IIT Delhi.

3

UN Impact Summit & World Cup

My journey reached the global stage at the 9th 1M1B Active Impact Summit at the United Nations, Geneva. Recognized as one of the World's Top 50 Changemakers in the Changemakers World Cup organised by the 1M1B organisation, feliciated at the UN headquarters Geneva, Switzerland.

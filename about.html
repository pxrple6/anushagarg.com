My Story

I'm a front-end developer, researcher, and writer based in Noida, India. Currently in IBDP Year 1 at Delhi Public School, RK Puram.

I was introduced to coding at six years old and started learning HTML and CSS at twelve. Since then, I've been building websites, running research, and finding a place where technology meets social impact.

What I Build

In 2024, I founded Annadatri — a student-led initiative to bridge food waste and malnutrition. The technology platform, Ahaavritti, became a filed Indian patent, two Scopus-indexed research papers, a Top 40 recognition at Samsung Solve for Tomorrow at IIT Delhi, and a presentation at the United Nations in Geneva, where I was named a Top 50 Global Changemaker.

I'm also exploring human-computer interaction and building new projects at the intersection of technology, art, and impact.

What I Write

Poetry and prose are not separate from my technical work — they are the same impulse: to make something that resonates. I was recognised by CBSE nationally for the Budding Authors Programme, and in 2025 I published my debut collection, The Slice I Never Shared.

I perform at storytelling events, compete in oratory contests, and write regularly — working across forms that connect personal reflection to broader social themes.

Current Focus

IBDP Year 1 at DPS RK Puram. Building new projects. Writing new poems. Looking for collaborations in human-computer interaction, technology for social good, and creative writing.

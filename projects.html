Annadatri & Ahaavritti

Annadatri is a student-led initiative bridging food waste and malnutrition in India. The mission: a zero-hunger community by connecting surplus food with those who need it most.

Ahaavritti is the technology platform — a full-stack web app connecting food donors with NGOs in real-time. It became the subject of a filed Indian patent (No. 202511083538) and two Scopus-indexed research papers.

Recognised Top 40 nationally at Samsung Solve for Tomorrow (IIT Delhi) and presented at the United Nations in Geneva, where I was named a Top 50 Global Changemaker.

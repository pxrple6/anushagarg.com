The Slice I Never Shared

by Anusha Garg

Some things are too tender to speak aloud — they live in the margins, in the pauses, in the slices of experience we keep entirely to ourselves. This debut collection invites you into those quiet, unshared spaces.

Written with clarity and courage, The Slice I Never Shared is deeply personal — raw, lyrical, and unmistakably its own. Each page is an act of trust between writer and reader.

The kind of book that makes you feel less alone in the particular loneliness of being human.

Author Anusha Garg
